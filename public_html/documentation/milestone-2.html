Milestone 2

Content Strategy

My site will be one page and consist of the following:

Jumbotron Welcome

A giant picture of me doing something with my name and navigation in the top right corner. A button on the bottom that says "click to go to about me"

About Me

This will be a written introduction about myself. It will include a list of relevant skills to web development and programming languages I am familiar with. I will mention my relevant education including coursework and certificates. I want to make this section as custom and specific to who I am and what I value as I can.

Projects Overview

Here I will include major projects I have completed including my capstone. Any side personal projects will also be included here. In the future I plan to use this section to showcase my best work and projects I've been included on during my career.

Contact Me

This will be a form section where anyone would be able to contact me via email. The main purpose for potential freelance work or inquiring about my skills. I might also include a downloadable resume here.

Footer with Socials

Links to my socials via icons including LinkedIn and GitHub. Maybe a back to the top button.
